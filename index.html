Weekly Workout Routine

Monday - Height & Mobility
Warm-up (5 min)
Arm Circles – 1 min
Leg Swings – 1 min
Neck & Shoulder Rolls – 1 min
Toe Touch & Stretch – 1 min
High Knees – 1 min

Main Workout (20 min)
Hanging Stretch – 2 min
Cobra Stretch & Cat-Cow – 2 min
Jumping Jacks – 2 min

Cool-down (5 min)
Deep Breathing – 1 min
Hamstring & Hip Stretch – 2 min
Elbow & Wrist Mobility – 2 min
Tuesday - Biceps & Strength
Warm-up (5 min)
Arm Circles – 1 min
Shoulder Rolls – 1 min
Wrist Rotations – 1 min
Light Jogging – 2 min

Main Workout (20 min)
Isometric Bicep Holds – 3 sets of 30 sec
Slow Negative Push-Ups – 3 sets of 10 reps
Wall or Incline Push-ups – 3 sets of 12 reps

Cool-down (5 min)
Deep Breathing – 1 min
Shoulder Stretch – 2 min
Wrist Mobility – 2 min
Wednesday - Weight Loss
Warm-up (5 min)
Jump Rope (Simulated) – 1 min
Arm Swings – 1 min
Side Lunges – 1 min
Marching in Place – 2 min

Main Workout (20 min)
Squats – 3 sets of 12 reps
Seated Leg Raises – 3 sets of 10 reps
Standing Side Leg Raises – 3 sets of 12 reps each side

Cool-down (5 min)
Deep Breathing – 1 min
Seated Hamstring Stretch – 2 min
Hip Mobility – 2 min
Thursday - Mobility & Biceps
Warm-up (5 min)
Leg Swings – 1 min
Neck Rolls – 1 min
Arm Circles – 1 min
Light Jogging – 2 min

Main Workout (20 min)
Hanging Stretch – 2 min
Wall Push-ups – 3 sets of 12 reps
Isometric Holds – 3 sets of 30 sec

Cool-down (5 min)
Shoulder Stretch – 2 min
Deep Breathing – 1 min
Wrist Rotations – 2 min
Friday - Strength & Weight Loss
Warm-up (5 min)
Shoulder Rolls – 1 min
Toe Touch – 1 min
Jumping Jacks – 1 min
Light Jogging – 2 min

Main Workout (20 min)
Slow Push-ups – 3 sets of 10 reps
Wall Push-ups – 3 sets of 12 reps
High Knees – 2 min

Cool-down (5 min)
Hamstring Stretch – 2 min
Deep Breathing – 1 min
Shoulder Rolls – 2 min
Saturday - Light Full-Body
Warm-up (5 min)
Toe Touch Stretch – 1 min
Arm Swings – 1 min
Marching in Place – 3 min

Main Workout (20 min)
Deep Breathing – 1 min
Seated Hamstring Stretch – 2 min
Shoulder Stretch – 2 min
Sunday - Rest Day
